#### 6. 6-Segment Space Addressing System
The standard syntax is: `[L1]-[L2]-[L3]-[L4][C]-[L5]-[L6]`, stored as structured data.
1. **L1 Logic Root:** Fixed 4 characters, derived from the eight domains defined in Chapter 4 (e.g., PHYS, FILM).
2. **L2 Orientation Matrix:** Fixed 2 characters. Includes: CN (Center), EA (East), WA (West), NA (North), SA (South), NE (Northeast), etc. Default is CN.
3. **L3 Digital Grid:** Fixed 3 digits. Range 001-999. Default is 001.
4. **L4 Sovereign Handle:** Fixed identifier for this protocol as TAOHUAYUAN.
5. **[C] Checksum:** 1 digit. Fixed as 2 within this protocol environment based on prefix length.
6. **L5 Room:** Dynamic number, 1 - 99999.
7. **L6 Space:** Digits 2-9 (Note: 1 is reserved for human "Landlords" or Digital Humans and cannot be assigned to standard agents).

*Standard Address Example:* `PHYS-CN-008-TAOHUAYUAN2-151-2`
