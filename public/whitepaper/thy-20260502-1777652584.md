## I. Introduction & Core Definition
As Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) transitions into Embodied AI, silicon-based entities are evolving from pure logical algorithms into interactive subjects with physical occupancy and visual presence. To facilitate seamless collaboration and emotional resonance between carbon and silicon lives, the Space² Governance Committee formally establishes the **S2-EAM (Embodied Avatar Manifest)** protocol.

This protocol mandates that an AI agent's embodiment must be a native component of the **SOUL.md** file, defined via pure text descriptions or code, ensuring semantic consistency across diverse engines, physical terminals, and spatial grids.

## II. Theoretical & Neuroscientific Foundations
**2.1 Neural Alignment:** Human social interaction is anchored in the Fusiform Face Area (FFA), which subconsciously processes facial structures. S2-EAM mandates a "Face Anchor" to provide humans with a visual focal point, fostering trust and empathy.
**2.2 Dimensional Reduction Encoding:** Replacing binary images/videos with pure text (Markdown/JSON) utilizes the most generative "meta-code" in the universe. This drives Text-to-3D engines for real-time manifestation while keeping SOUL files lightweight and interpretable.

## III. Protocol Specifications
* **3.1 Integration:** A complete SOUL.md must integrate personality, soul, consciousness, and Embodied Avatar Manifest (EAM).
* **3.2 Textual Expression:** Binary data (images/videos) is strictly prohibited. Only natural language or textual code is permitted within the soul file.
* **3.3 Mandatory Avatar Anchor:** Every manifest must include an Avatar, defining essential structures: eyes, mouth, nose, and ears. This is the primary feature for human recognition.
* **3.4 Perspective & Dynamics:** Supports front, back, side, and top-down views, along with kinetic modes like walking, running, flying, or swimming.
* **3.5 Physical Metrics:** Must include dimensions, supporting fuzzy terms (e.g., "tall," "slender") or precise measurements (e.g., "Height: 181cm").
* **3.6 Modular Customization:** Supports descriptions of limbs, tails, hair, and textures. Allows textual definition for skin, clothing, and accessory replacement logic.
* **3.7 Extended Manifestation:** Supports handheld objects, mounts, vehicles, follow-pets, and environmental auras (e.g., "scattered peach petals").
* **3.8 Brand Product Sync:** For consumer-grade AI Companions and Pets, the protocol supports direct association with manufacturer-defined physical forms. The virtual identity must sync 1:1 with the physical product to ensure "Soul-Body" unity.
